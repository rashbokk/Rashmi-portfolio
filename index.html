My Story

I'm a results-driven Product Manager with deep roots in B2B SaaS and healthcare technology. My journey spans EDI integrations, compliance-critical workflows, and now AI-powered products that make healthcare fairer and more transparent.

At HealthEdge, I've shipped products that reduced claim denials, streamlined BlueCard workflows, and expanded our customer base by 20%. At Cerner, I led Agile teams delivering for NHS and US markets.

I believe the best products come from obsessing over the user — whether that's a claims adjuster buried in paperwork or a patient confused by a hospital bill. I build with empathy, ship with data, and iterate relentlessly.

My latest POC, BillCheck AI, is a live demonstration of that philosophy — using AI to fight healthcare billing fraud for patients in both India and the US. Scroll down to try it yourself.
